Business Registration & Setup

Register business in Quebec, obtain NEQ number, choose appropriate legal structure (sole proprietorship, corporation, partnership).

Identify & Hire Technical Guarantor

Find experienced HVAC technician with C of Q and gas licenses to serve as technical guarantor. They handle technical competency requirements.

Complete RBQ business administration examination or equivalent recognized training program to serve as business guarantor.

Submit RBQ License Application

Apply for appropriate HVAC subclasses (15.1, 15.7, 15.10) with both business and technical guarantors in place.
